Accident & Emergency Care

Life is unpredictable, but our 24/7 emergency services are always ready. We're here to provide timely, compassionate care when you need it most, ensuring your safety and well-being.

At Hayat Hospital, we are fully equipped to handle any medical emergency or accident, with our 24-hour accident and emergency unit and ambulance service. Our experienced emergency management teams are always ready to provide top-quality care and treatment to patients in crisis.

The Hayat Ambulance fleet is purpose-built with remote access patient monitoring systems to stabilize critical patients. This enables first responders to send vital ECG information to Hayat Heart Centre for analysis by a consultant, so treatment can be applied en route to the hospital.
